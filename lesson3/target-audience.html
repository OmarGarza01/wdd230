Target Audience

Our target audience are the common People of South Texas. Theses people are your average mom or dad who love to go out to the park and enjoy the sun with there family. People who enjoy the outdoors and Valley Weather.

Personas

Persona 1: Soccer mom who takes there children to practice.
Persona 2: Your average joe who loves to fire up the pit for a nice weekend cookout.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: There is a major high school football game between rival school. You check your phone to see if its likely to get cold at night and according to the website seem like it is so you take your best warm clothing to the football game.
Scenario 2: Your having a birthday party outside in the back yard and you see that the wether is going to be nice and sunny so you fire up the BBQ pit.
Scenario 3: A new virus call covid-20 immerge and the community informed when they check the weather for the day.
Scenario 4: You open up the weather site and you realize that there is going to be a city festival with live music and games for the children to play and enjoy the nice afternoon weather. And you end up going and enjoy some time with the family.
Scenario 5: There is a hurricane 3 days away and you get informed and alerted to makes the necessary preparations to move to a safer place or to stock up on supply.
